Business & Economic Insights

Analysis of 494,954 Airbnb listings using Linear Regression (R²=0.8799, RMSE=$50.40) and K-Means Clustering (K=3)

Key Findings

📈

Property Capacity Drives Price

Bedrooms is the strongest numerical predictor (coef: +42.67), followed by Accommodates (+11.16) and Bathrooms (+9.39). Interestingly, Beds has a slight negative coefficient (−1.94), suggesting more beds without more rooms signals shared/budget setups.

⭐

Reviews Equal Revenue

Higher review scores correlate with higher pricing (coefficient: +1.58 per std deviation). Mean review score across listings is 92.90. Guest experience quality is a direct revenue lever.

🏠

Entire Home Premium

Mean price: Entire home/apt $173.43 vs Private room $79.16 vs Shared room $60.50. Entire homes account for 306,113 listings (62.9% of data). Room type coefficients: Entire −$87.64, Private −$100.75, Shared −$111.46 (relative to intercept $217.76).

📍

Location Is Everything

Top regions by avg price: Indre By ($692.63), Hiller Highlands ($650.00), Southern ($643.26), Vesterbro-Kongens Enghave ($635.53). With 2,335 unique neighbourhoods, location coefficients range from +$302 to −$261.

🔬

Three Market Segments

K-Means clustering (K=3) on 360,764 listings: Cluster 2 (282,573 listings, avg $87.66), Cluster 1 (61,840, avg $220.79, 6.48 guests avg), Cluster 0 (16,351, avg $613.88, $508.86/bedroom).

⚖️

Supply-Demand Dynamics

Availability 365 has a positive coefficient (+2.74), suggesting more available listings tend to be priced higher (hosts pricing high may get fewer bookings). Number of Reviews is slightly negative (−1.89), indicating frequently-booked budget listings accumulate more reviews.

💰

Dynamic Pricing Opportunity

Hosts can optimize revenue by adjusting prices based on seasonality, local events, and competitor analysis. Charge premiums during peak demand and competitive rates off-peak.

⚠️

Risk: Over vs. Underpricing

Overpricing leads to vacancy and negative reviews. Underpricing leaves revenue on the table and sets a hard-to-raise baseline. Data-driven pricing provides the optimal balance.
